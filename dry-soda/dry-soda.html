Summer Mocktails You Need to Try

We've all been there. Hot summer day, long day at work, craving something refreshing (and hydrating) to sip on. Sometimes a cocktail just doesn't do it for us. A mocktail however...now that hits the spot. Sparkling, non-alcoholic beverages are in right now, and we're jumping on the bandwagon just in time for summer. If you're looking for a new family-friendly recipe, we've got you covered with these top-notch picks from bloggers across the globe.

Recipe by Savor the Flavour

Non Alcoholic Strawberry Citrus Blush

This non alcoholic strawberry citrus blush is a great way to show off fresh local strawberries this spring and summer. The drink contains pureed and sliced strawberries for a good strawberry flavor, in addition to three kinds of citrus juice."

- Brooke & Emma, Savor the Flavour
